Access Control System

An access control system is a security solution designed to regulate who can enter or use a particular environment or resource. It typically involves authentication mechanisms such as passwords, biometric scans, or access cards, along with authorization protocols defining permissions for different users.

CCTV System

A CCTV (Closed Circuit Television) system comprises cameras strategically placed to monitor and record activity within an area. These cameras feed video footage to a central system for live monitoring or recording, enhancing security and providing a means to review events retrospectively.

Video door phone system { VDP }

A Video Door Phone (VDP) system integrates a camera and intercom at the entrance of a property, allowing occupants to see and communicate with visitors remotely. It enables users to verify the identity of visitors before granting them access, enhancing security and convenience. VDP systems are commonly used in residential complexes, offices, and commercial buildings to monitor entry points and control access efficiently.

Website development

Website development involves creating and designing web pages to establish an online presence for individuals, businesses, or organizations. It encompasses tasks such as coding, graphic design, and content creation to ensure a visually appealing and functional website.
